Nearing the end of our conversation we ask about nearby stores and they tell us about gnome depot, blood bath and beyond, and 13 dwarven guys. As we enter the church we note 7 bodies, 4 of which have the rings we were looking for from the quest given by [[Captain Flynt Locke]] before. We note the church's symbol, upwards fist with a sun behind it, and head toward the back of the church where a shriek emerges. having the [[Rod of Magic Detection]] [[Aedric]] goes first with [[Ken]] next, then [[Lobis]], with [[Theric]] brining up the rear.
