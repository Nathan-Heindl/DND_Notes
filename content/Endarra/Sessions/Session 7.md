# Non-Combat
***
The party heads north in pursuit of the quarantine escapees. Two charred horse corpses are noticed on the side of the road, and further north are the two guards [[Varian]] originally sent. [[Aedric]] wildshapes into a horse, leaving two open horses. The party continues onward now with the two mercenaries in tow, soon to find the runaways. A man and a woman state they are from [[Dun Boldor]], simply ferrying grain. 

# Combat
***
As the party converse and attempt to peacefully ascertain the scheme, [[Ken]] is stabbed in the back, taking 8 damage. The melee mercenary with the team fires a crossbow bolt on reaction, dealing 1 damage to the cultist. The second [[Conjurer]] summons a sprawling bone serpent right in front of [[Mustel]]. [[Aedric]] rides up to the second cultist and crits, trampling them for 7 damage. The mercenary [[Larian]] is still with him as they ride out of melee range. [[Mustel]] grazes the serpent for 2 damage, but it retaliated by beaming 16 psychic damage onto him as well as charming them. [[Ken]] casts burning hands for 16 damage on the second cultist, and [[Larian]] holds the first cultist in place before they can attack [[Ken]] again. [[Theric]] fires a guiding bolt at the second cultist, quickly following up by summoning a spiritual weapon and impaling the foe, killing him. The melee mercenary with the party fights his fears and slices at the skeletal serpent, unfortunately missing. The first and now only living cultist is held in place and cannot escape. The charmed [[Mustel]] will attack the mercenary closest to him, grazing him for 2 damage and greatly startling him. [[Aedric]] will drop off [[Larian]] who is still channeling hold person and slams the serpent with its hooves, dealing a surprisingly devastating 22 damage, as the creature is susceptible to bludgeoning damage. The serpent positions itself for an arcing lightening attack, but in response the now un-charmed [[Mustel]] will take the opportunity to attack the creature. Divine retribution courses through [[Mustel]]'s fire and steel, critically striking the creature and fully beheading it with 47 damage. It clatters to the ground decimated. [[Ken]] now turns to the last cultist, commanding [[Scrappy]] to sink a blade into his shoulder while he's still stunned, dealing 22 damage. [[Theric]] effortlessly ushers his spirit weapon over to the held cultist, cracking their head with the blunt side and knocking them out, ending the combat

# Non-Combat
***
The party loots the enemies, each gaining 20 gold. They inspect the cargo and it appears to be a sickly green stash of wheat and grains. No magic is detected on or around it. Using the sending stone, [[Varian]] and [[Ken]] discuss, and the party will leave the cultist with [[Larian]] and [[Patrick]] (the other mercenary) and await reinforcements, while they themselves will again be destined for [[Thunderkeep]]. [[Ken]] raises a [[Kens Zombie|Zombie]], and the party parts ways with their ephemeral comrades. As the day passes and night falls, it begins to lightly snow and the party seems to finally lay eyes upon [[Thunderkeep]]. High walls with lights and watchtowers stand in front of them. The situation that occurred in [[Drenchport]] with [[Targ]] and [[Shell]] is described to a man at the gate and he lets the party in without issue. The demographic of this city is eclectic, Goblins, Kobolds, a humanoid bird species that they don't recognize, and even a Hill Giant are present, all coexisting. The party is lead into the central fortress. Most everyone is working on weapons, armor, or some other militarized good. They pass through a huge door, and inside is a vast war room with a surprisingly clean and armored Stone Giant at the head, and [[Targ]] at his side. Commander [[Hundar]], the Stone Giant, presses the party by asking their usefulness. [[Hundar]] seems oddly intrigued by both [[Mustel]] and [[Aedric]], but is interrupted by echoing bells throughout the fortress. The party is moved down a hallway and up a manual elevator with [[Targ]], up to the top of the wall. A large and varied array of siege weapons can be seen, as well as [[Hundar]]'s 20 foot figure readying for the incursion. It is described that smaller scout groups are used beyond the wall to distract smaller groups of undead so that the full breadth of a hoard does not hit the walls. [[Hundar]] gestures that that is what the party will be doing now, and success will officially welcome them into the city. The party finds their designated bunker and anxiously awaits the incoming undead. 

# Combat
***
The first of the shambling zombies comes into view, then more, dozens more following. [[Skeleton]]s are within the hoard. The party no longer can see anything but undead outside their bunker. Rocks from siege weapons begin crashing into the hoard. As yells from other scout groups start to be heard, the party decides to ring their bunker's bell, drawing the attention of a wave of [[Skeleton]]s. [[Aedric]] summons a growth of thorns, and the team rolls initiative. [[Theric]] fells a fiend with his spirit weapon from range, and [[Aedric]] follows up by felling another with a ray of frost. After this, two [[Skeletal War Horse]]s and two [[Skeletal Minotaur]] appear from the horde and dash at the party, immediately making it into melee range. [[Ken]]  stuns both the horse and the minotaur closest to him with a hideous laughter. An odd, red eyed skeleton rushes forward and lobs two fire strikes at [[Aedric]], and while he takes 15 damage, he maintains concentration on his spikes. [[Mustel]] dashes ahead and destroys the stunned [[Skeletal War Horse]] with a single zealous strike. The hoard advances, with a swath of them impaling themselves on the spike growth. Many of survivors congregate around [[Aedric]], now reaching an overwhelming amount of opposition. From across the battlefield from the Thunder Keep wall, a ballista bolt soars through the air and two of the [[Skeleton]]s, reducing them to dust. A wave of armed [[Zombie]] has appeared behind the skeletons. The other war horse is collapsed by [[Theric]] , while a [[Zombie]] fires two arrows at [[Mustel]]  dealing 15 damage. The standing [[Skeletal Minotaur]] slams [[Aedric]] for 19 damage, but he doesn't drop concentration. Two [[Zombie Ogre]]s have now joined the battle, dashing up to the party and leading the charge for the dozens of [[Zombie]] infantry. [[Ken]] attempts to blast the stunned [[Skeletal Minotaur]], but unfortunately [[Skeleton]]s and [[Zombie]]s are immune to poison damage. The [[Red-eyed Skeleton]] fires off two more bolts at [[Aedric]]. [[Aedric]] slips and luckily dodges the critical fire bolt. [[Mustel]] then uses sentinels shield on [[Aedric]], before a wave of [[Zombie]]s march into the spikes which also impale them. A lone [[Skeleton]] slashes at [[Aedric]] , dealing 6 damage, downing him and dropping the field of spikes. Another ballista bolt arcs across the battle, tearing the guts out of one the [[Zombie Ogre]]s with 32 damage, yet somehow it still stands. [[Theric]] tolls the dead, igniting and detonating the [[Red-eyed Skeleton]] which launches flaming debris at everyone close by. He then heals [[Aedric]] for 11 health, bringing him back to his feet. [[Aedric]] uses a greater healing potion and wild shapes into a crocodile. The standing [[Skeletal Minotaur]] swings at [[Aedric]]  dealing 12 damage. The stunned [[Skeletal Minotaur]] finally stands up and gets ready to charge, taking an attack of opportunity from [[Mustel]]. The attack hits but the [[Skeletal Minotaur]] is unfazed, rushing at [[Mustel]] and goring her for 23 damage, instantly knocking her unconscious. [[Aedric]] is attacked again by the ballista-struck [[Zombie Ogre]] for 6 damage, dropping his crocodile form. [[Ken]] moves to block [[Mustel]] from harm, using a healing potion to get him back on his feet. [[Willy Wonder]] also strikes at the [[Zombie Ogre]], dealing 3 damage. [[Mustel]] uses lay on hands to heal himself back to full strength, right before an artillery strike flattens one of the [[Zombie Ogre]]s. [[Theric]] misses an attempt to toll the dead, but moves to switch places with [[Aedric]]. [[Aedric]] steps behind [[Theric]] and casts shatter, dealing 16 damage to the [[Skeletal Minotaur]] and crumbling the remaining [[Zombie]]. The [[Skeletal Minotaur]] and [[Zombie Ogre]] swing on [[Theric]] with little effect, while [[Ken]] stabs at the bloodied [[Skeletal Minotaur]] that downed [[Mustel]], finally felling it with his trident. [[Mustel]] rushes forward with renewed vigor and decides to "swing on he bitch ass", killing the final [[Skeletal Minotaur]]. The final combatants are in disarray, [[Aedric]] and [[Ken]] both felling the final infantry [[Zombie]] with their tridents. [[Mustel]] stabs the final [[Zombie Ogre]] in the jugular, spraying black ichor and ending the confrontation.
